Projects Overview

My Background

I'm Kase McCormick, a data analytics and data science professional with a passion for turning raw data into meaningful, actionable insights. My experience spans the full analytics pipeline — from data collection and cleaning to visualization and deployment — with a strong emphasis on practical, business-focused solutions.

My academic and professional journey has given me hands-on exposure to statistical analysis, machine learning concepts, and enterprise reporting. I have a particular focus on the Microsoft Power Platform, where I develop custom applications and interactive dashboards that help organizations make data-driven decisions more efficiently.

Data Analytics

In my analytics work, I specialize in exploring, transforming, and visualizing datasets to uncover trends and support decision-making. I'm experienced with building end-to-end reporting pipelines — from connecting to data sources and modeling relationships, to designing dashboards that are intuitive for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

A core part of my analytics toolkit is Power BI, where I design interactive reports featuring KPIs, slicers, drill-throughs, and custom visuals. I leverage DAX for calculated measures and Power Query to clean and shape data before it ever reaches the report layer.

Data Science

Beyond analytics, I apply data science principles to build predictive and descriptive models. My coursework and project work include regression analysis, classification, and exploratory data analysis — primarily using Python with libraries like pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, and Matplotlib.

I enjoy bridging the gap between data science outputs and real-world tools, making model results accessible through dashboards and apps that non-technical users can interact with directly.
